Nigeria's Cleanest Fuel Alternative — Now Affordable for Everyone

CNG is one of the world's safest vehicle fuels. Tanks are pressure-tested to withstand extreme conditions. Unlike petrol, CNG disperses rapidly into the air if there's a leak — it doesn't pool and ignite. All our installations follow Nigeria Fire Regulation and EU Standards.
Most petrol-engine vehicles can be converted — including cars, Danfo buses, Keke NAPEP tricycles, and commercial trucks. Your vehicle will first undergo a free eligibility inspection at one of our certified partner centers.
Based on current Nigerian fuel pricing, CNG costs approximately 40–63% less per kilometre compared to PMS (petrol). For a high-mileage driver covering 200km/day, the savings can exceed ₦150,000 per month, meaning most customers recover conversion costs within 2–4 months.
All our converted vehicles are bi-fuel — meaning your vehicle can seamlessly switch between CNG and petrol at the press of a button. You're never stranded, and you always have petrol as a backup while Nigeria's CNG station network continues to expand rapidly.
